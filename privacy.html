Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025 · Written in plain English

The short version — plain English

  • We use your location to show you local weather — we never store it
  • Your horse profiles are saved on your device only, not on our servers
  • We don't use cookies or tracking right now
  • We don't sell your data — ever
  • If you click a product link, we may earn a small affiliate commission at no cost to you
  • You can delete your horse profiles any time by clearing your browser data
01

Who we are

SteedSize is a free equestrian tool that helps horse owners choose the right rug weight and size based on local weather conditions and their horse's individual profile. SteedSize is operated by Francesca, based in the UK.

If you have any questions about this policy, please get in touch at francesca@steedsize.co.uk

02

What data we use — and why

We keep things simple. Here's exactly what SteedSize uses:

📍 Your location
Used to fetch local weather via Open-Meteo. Never stored on our servers. Only used in the moment you request it.
🐴 Your horse profiles
Saved on your device using browser localStorage. We never see this data — it stays on your phone or computer.
🌤️ Weather data
Fetched from Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com), a free weather API. No personal data is sent to them beyond your coordinates.
🍪 Cookies
We don't currently use any cookies or third-party tracking tools. If this changes, we'll update this policy and add a cookie notice.
03

Your profiles — where they live & how to back them up

Everything you add about your horse — their details, what you do together, their personality and any photo — is saved only on the device you're using, in your browser's own storage. It never leaves your phone or computer, and we never see it.

That keeps things completely private, but there are two practical things worth knowing:

It won't follow you to another device. A profile you create on your phone won't appear on your laptop, because the two don't share storage.

It can be lost if you clear your browser data. Clearing your browsing history, cache or site data — or using private / incognito mode — can remove your saved profiles.

So you never lose a profile you've put time into, every profile has an Export backup button on its page. This downloads a small file containing that horse's details and photo, which you can keep somewhere safe. If you ever need it back — on a new phone, or after clearing your browser — use Import a backup on the profile page to restore it.

We're planning optional cloud accounts in future, which will let profiles sync across your devices automatically. Until then, exporting a backup is the way to keep your data safe.

04

Affiliate links & how we make money

SteedSize is free to use. To keep it that way, product links on the site may be affiliate links — meaning if you click through and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission from the retailer.

This costs you nothing extra. The price you pay is exactly the same whether you come through SteedSize or go directly to the retailer.

We only recommend products we'd genuinely consider for our own horses. Affiliate relationships never influence our rug weight recommendations — those are based purely on weather data and your horse's profile.

05

Third party services we use

Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) — provides our weather data. When you request a weather check, your approximate coordinates are sent to Open-Meteo to retrieve your local forecast. Open-Meteo is a privacy-friendly, open-source weather API that does not store personal data.

Nominatim / OpenStreetMap — used to convert your coordinates into a place name (e.g. "Newmarket, Suffolk"). Again, no personal data is stored by this service.

Google Fonts — used to load our fonts. Google may log a request when the fonts load. See Google's privacy policy for details.

06

Your rights

Because we don't collect or store your personal data on our servers, there's very little for us to share or delete. However, under UK GDPR you have the right to:

Access your data — ask us what data we hold (the honest answer is: almost none)

Delete your horse profiles — clear your browser's localStorage at any time to remove all saved horse data from your device

Opt out of location access — deny location permission in your browser and enter your location manually instead

Contact us — with any privacy concerns at francesca@steedsize.co.uk

07

Young users

SteedSize is designed for horse owners of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone. As no data is stored on our servers, SteedSize is safe for young riders to use — though we'd always encourage younger users to have a parent or guardian nearby when using any online tool.

08

Changes to this policy

If we add new features — such as analytics, newsletter signups, or a user account system — we'll update this policy before those features go live and add any required notices (such as a cookie banner) at that point.

The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.

Questions about this policy?

We're happy to explain anything in plain English. Drop us a line at francesca@steedsize.co.uk and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
