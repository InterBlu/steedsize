Our story

Built by a horse owner, for horse owners

SteedSize started with a simple question on a cold winter morning — and grew into something much bigger.

Francesca
Founder, SteedSize · hobbyist equestrian
How it started

I came to horses as an adult learner. That changed everything.

I didn't grow up around horses. There was no family tradition, no years of pony club, no one to pass down the instinctive knowledge that experienced equestrians seem to carry so naturally. When I started riding five years ago, I came to it completely fresh — and I quickly discovered just how much there is to learn.

"Standing at the field gate on a cold January morning, I genuinely didn't know whether my horse needed a rug — or which one. I just guessed. And I hated that."

Rugging felt like one of the most stressful decisions I faced as a new horse owner. Too warm, and you risk sweating and skin issues. Too cold, and you're worried all night. The guidance I found online was conflicting, complicated, or buried in forums full of jargon I didn't yet understand.

I wanted something simple. Something that looked at the actual weather tonight — my weather, in my location — and told me what my horse needed. Not a generic chart. Not a forum thread from 2011. A proper, personalised recommendation based on real conditions.

That tool didn't exist. So I built it.

What we believe

Good tools should be free and simple.

🌤️
Weather-first
Rug decisions should be based on actual forecast data, not guesswork. We use live local weather so your recommendation is always accurate for tonight.
🐴
Horse-first
Every horse is different. Coat type, living situation, breed and temperament all affect what they need. SteedSize accounts for all of it.
📏
Honest sizing
Rug sizing varies wildly between brands. Our size guide walks you through measuring properly so you buy with confidence, not guesswork.
💛
Free, always
SteedSize is free for horse owners. We earn a small commission if you buy through our links — at no extra cost to you. That's how we keep the lights on.
🐴

Our mission: confident horse owners

Whether you've had horses for thirty years or thirty days, you deserve a tool that meets you where you are — and helps you make the best decision for your horse, every single day.

Where we're headed

Starting with rugs. Growing with you.

SteedSize launched with rugging because it's one of the most common, most stressful and most weather-dependent decisions horse owners face every day. But it's just the beginning.

Bridle fitting, saddle sizing, feed guidance — the same principle applies everywhere: personalised, simple tools that help real horse owners make better decisions. That's what SteedSize is building, one tool at a time.

If you have ideas, feedback or just want to say hello — I'd genuinely love to hear from you.
